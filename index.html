About Me

✧ the story

I'm a UI/UX designer who focuses on creating simple, user-centered digital experiences. I enjoy turning complex ideas into clear, functional designs that users can easily understand and interact with. My journey started as a UI/UX intern at CODE IT, where I built a strong foundation in design principles, tools, and real-world project work.


Currently, I work as an Intern Supervisor and Tutor, where I guide and support upcoming designers. I review their work, help them improve their design thinking, and ensure they follow user-centered and consistent design practices. I believe good design is not just about how things look, but how they work.

"Good design balances emotion and logic — it's where clarity meets delight."
